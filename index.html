All About Me

My name is Victoria Alvarez.
For as long as I can remember, I had a passion for animals., specifically cats. I payed close attention to their movements and structures, and would scribble down their appearances on a piece of lined paper stolen from my grandfather's office. I was very young when my mother gave me my first set of crayola crayons, that pack of six was gone by the following week! All I would ever do was draw what I saw around me, or my pets. I also had an obsession over Tom & Jerry. It was the first cartoon I had ever watched, and to this day it remains my most treasured memory. 

MORE

To master Adobe Photoshop

Adobe Photoshop was my first ever artistic application, and my favorite. I believe that if I continue to use it effectively, I can read it like a book.

Enjoy photography as a hobby

I love to take pictures of nature and animals, and want to travel around the globe doing just that.

Succeed in a role as an artist

I want to be able to not just be an artist, but to pay for that as a seperate job. I will have to study many techniques. 

Progress more in literature

My favorite subject is language arts, and making stories and backgrounds about different characters of mine are something I really enjoy.

When I started school, I remembered my first grade teacher asking what I want to be when I grow up, and I said that I wanted to be an artist for Disney or Dreamworks. She thought that it was way too advanced and that I would never be able to make it. I did not let this get in my way. As I made my way into fourth grade, I met Janessa Blanco. She was an artist, just like me. We teamed up and started making commissions for our fellow classmates. To this day she remains my best friend, and we have stuck together creating and selling our artworks. 
Last year, I took a trip to Key West. I was amazed by the colors and history behind the historical buildings and sites. I decided to start taking pictures. I have developed a love for photography, and now I travel to take photos of anything interesting I lay eyes on. Recently, I went to Captiva, and noticed the varieties of different species of animals and plants. I started taking pictures of birds, palm trees, and sunsets. I had thought of an idea then, to create fictional characters off the pictures I took. Now, I have a lot of characters of my own.
I joined the magnet strand technology production because of the passion I developed for art and photography, and I hope to do well in the nearing future.
